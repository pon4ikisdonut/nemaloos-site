Frequently asked questions

What release format does NemaloOS use?

We follow the Year.Month "Codename" scheme. The current stable release is 25.10 "Donut".

Which package managers are available?

Both pacman and dnf are available out of the box. Check Discord for advice on mirrors and building your own repositories.

How do I install the system?

The Calamares installer walks you through the setup in just a handful of steps. If you run into questions, open the #install-issues channel on Discord.
