Report a bug

Please describe the issue in detail and include logs whenever possible. All reports are handled through our Discord server.

Open the #bug-report channel

How to submit

  1. Join the NemaloOS Discord server.
  2. In the Support category, open the #bug-reports channel.
  3. Create a new post.
  4. Describe the problem, include your NemaloOS version (for example, 25.10 "Donut"), and list reproduction steps.
  5. Attach logs from /var/log or the output of journalctl when relevant.

For urgent issues, contact the moderators directly on Discord.
